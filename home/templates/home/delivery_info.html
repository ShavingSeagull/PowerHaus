How much is delivery?

Delivery is £1.20 (1.20 GBP) per item in the cart, regardless of what it is or where it's going. Delivery charges are applied after any discounts are applied.

What are the delivery times like?

Naturally, take these timeframes with a slight grain of salt, though the typical delivery times for the various regions are as follows:

  • UK - 2-3 Business days
  • Europe - 5-7 Business days
  • Americas - 12-15 Business days
  • Asia - 18-20 Business days
  • Oceania/Australasia - 20-22 Business days

Do you have a refund policy?

Absolutely! If there is an issue with your purchase, we'd be happy to sort it out for you. Just be aware that with certain items, such as underwear, we can only accommodate refunds for items that arrive damanged, for obvious reasons. When you get in touch with regards to your order, give us as much detail about the issue as possible, so that we can process the request as quickly as possible.
